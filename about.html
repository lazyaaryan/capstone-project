About Viber

Where developers collaborate, learn, and ship together

Viber connects developers to collaborate, learn, and ship. Built by coders, for coders, we're reimagining how development teams communicate and build software in real-time.

Our Mission

To create the most powerful, intuitive platform for developers to connect, share knowledge, and build the future of technology together. We believe great software is built through collaboration, not isolation.

What We Do

Viber provides a platform where developers can collaborate in real-time, share code snippets, get help from the community, and build amazing projects together. We focus on simplicity, speed, and security.
